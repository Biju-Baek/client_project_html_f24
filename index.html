Study Area in Campus

Check out the list of differnent study areas in the campus and find your favorite spot:

Hatcher Graduate Library

Harlan Hatcher Graduate Library is the university's largest and most historic library, offering an extensive collection of research materials, quiet study spaces, and stunning architecture that reflects the rich academic tradition of the University of Michigan.

Kresge Business Administration Library

Located next to Ross Business Building, enjoy authentic global cuisine, cozy study spaces, and an inspiring collection of resources at the Kresge Library, a perfect spot for business students and the broader university community.

Mental Health

Maintaining your mental well-being is crucial for thriving in graduate school. Here are resources to support your mental health:

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS):

Access individual counseling, group therapy, workshops, and crisis intervention services.

Wellness Coaching:

Work with a wellness coach to develop strategies for managing stress, time, and overall well-being.

Mindfulness Resources:

Participate in mindfulness and meditation sessions to cultivate a calm and focused mind.

Support Groups:

Join peer support groups for shared experiences and collective coping strategies.

Life Outside the Classroom

Graduate school is more than just academics. It's important to cultivate a balanced life. Here are some resources to help you enjoy life outside the classroom:

  1. Student Organizations: Join one of the many student organizations to connect with peers who share your interests and passions.
  2. Arts and Culture: Explore UM's vibrant arts scene with events, exhibitions, and performances happening year-round.
  3. Career Services: Utilize career counseling, job search resources, and networking opportunities to prepare for your professional future.
  4. Community Engagement: Get involved with local volunteering opportunities and community service projects to make a positive impact.
  5. Housing and Dining:Find information on on-campus housing options and dining services to ensure a comfortable living experience.
